## About

### Project Background

Developed in collaboration with [The Glasgow Women's Library](https://womenslibrary.org.uk/) as part of the We Make Museums Project, "Build & Connect" is a physical data puzzle designed to accompany the museum's July exhibition in 2025. The project draws on direct conversations with community members, providing a tangible way to contextualise their interests, key themes, and the exhibited objects.
