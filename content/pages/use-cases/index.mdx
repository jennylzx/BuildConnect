## Use cases

### We Make Museums Project Exhibit

The "Build & Connect" exhibit was featured at the Museum Project Exhibition for three months. The installation was designed as an open-access experience, inviting all museum visitors to interact with the piece.

### Workshop with the GWL community

During the exhibition, the project community held a workshop to explore the concept of reflection through physical material. Members of the We Make Museums Project were invited to collaboratively design and build a new "Build & Connect" puzzle from scratch, using the tactile pieces to facilitate group discussion and reflection.
