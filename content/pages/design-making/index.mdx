# Design & Making

## Design Objectives

1. To design the puzzle pieces in a ways that would allow each piece to represent one theme in written form.
2. To embed the hierarchy of low-level and overarching themes, and
3. To allow for the representation of images representing exhibit objects to be connected to themes.

## Final Puzzle Piece Design

The final system comprises three distinct sizes designed for infinite expansion:

- **Small Pieces:** Represent low-level themes.
- **Mid-size Pieces:** Represent overarching themes.
- **Large Pieces:** Represent physical objects selected by community members for the exhibition.
