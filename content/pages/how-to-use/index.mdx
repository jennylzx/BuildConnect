# How to Use It

There are no rigid instructions for the puzzle, as the system is designed to be highly adaptable to specific community contexts.

The fundamental value lies in the collaborative construction of data. Users are encouraged to input information by writing or drawing directly onto the pieces, then moving, adding, or removing them to visualize evolving relationships and narratives.
