# Design & Making

## Design Objectives

1. **Thematic Representation:** Designing puzzle pieces that allow individual themes to be expressed in written form.
2. **Structural Hierarchy:** Embedding a visual system to distinguish between granular, low-level details and overarching themes.
3. **Visual Connection:** Creating a method to link physical exhibition objects (represented via images) to their corresponding themes.

## Design Iterations

- Testing various puzzle shapes and connection styles. Refining the "tabs and blanks" system to ensure interlocking.

## Final Puzzle Piece Design

The final system comprises three distinct sizes designed for infinite expansion:

- **Small Pieces:** Represent low-level, specific themes.
- **Mid-size Pieces:** Represent overarching, broader themes.
- **Large Pieces:** Represent physical objects selected by community members for the exhibition.

## Fabrication Process

- **Method:** Laser cutting.
